How To Appeal

You must first pick a method to appeal your ticket(This can also be seen directly on the ticket):

  • Submitting a hearing request online

    1. Go to the hearing request website
    2. Search for your ticket with the information on your ticket
    3. Submit a hearing request for the ticket

  • Contesting the ticket by mail

    1. Write a statement that establishes your defense for the ticket
    2. Enclose any evidence you may have(indicate ticket number and make
      sure you are using copies of evidence)
    3. Mail the statement with the pre-addressed envelope the ticket came with

  • Contest Tickets In-Person

    1. Request an in-person hearing by calling 312.744.7277
    2. You will receive a notice that notes details of your hearing
    3. Present your evidence at the hearing.
